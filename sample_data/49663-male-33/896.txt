Ban on gay marriages clears Ohio Senate    Columbus - A divided Ohio Senate approved a bill banning gay marriage Wednesday after a lengthy debate about religion, civil rights and health insurance benefits for gay couples.   The bill, approved 18-15, declares that same-sex marriages are against the "strong public policy of the state." It would prevent Ohio from recognizing same-sex unions, regardless of where they are per- formed, and it would prevent the state from extending the legal benefits of marriage to unmarried couples.   urlLink Full story
