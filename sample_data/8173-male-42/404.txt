urlLink A Bioweapon Worse Than Anthrax?  Anthrax and smallpox are on everyone's radar as potentially devastating bioweapons. But a seldom-heard-of bacteria is also on the government's A-list of bioterror threats.   The pathogen that causes tularemia is extremely infectious, experts say, and no vaccine is available to the general public. But researchers disagree over whether tularemia poses as serious a threat as other potential bioterror agents.
