urlLink Apocalyptic Representations of Late Capitalism  Rollerball (1975) speculates upon what mode of capitalism would replace the ailing Fordist mantra. Picking up on a growing unease the public felt with multi-national companies during the mid 70s, it predicts a world run purely by corporations, and explores the means by which this Corporate Society contains the social, and psychological side effects of capitalism that ultimately threaten to undermine it.
