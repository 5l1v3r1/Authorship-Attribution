urlLink The News Media Could Be Our Weakest Link  We need the media to keep challenging the government, because that friction makes us all stronger. But uninformed journalists can't effectively question authority. For example, well-meaning but misguided government efforts to classify too much information could harm national security by slowing the delivery of research results beneficial to society. And unless the public is well-informed, it won't know how to analyze the issues and know how to assess the information being provided by its leaders.
