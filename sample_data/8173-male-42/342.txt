urlLink Sniffing Out Bioterrorism Threat  Nightmare scenarios envisioned by bioterrorism experts include a small plane flying above a community, releasing anthrax or other germs over large numbers of people. Depending on the winds, thousands of people could become ill, yet it could take days to figure out what happened.  The sooner health officials detect a bioterrorism incident, the sooner they can properly treat victims with vaccines or antibiotics and protect others who might become infected.
