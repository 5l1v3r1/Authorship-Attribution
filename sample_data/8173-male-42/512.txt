urlLink Have you seen this man?  One of the weirdest rumours about Pynchon, was that he was the Unabomber. On the face of it, there are certain philosophical parallels between Pynchon and the real Unabomber. But of course the crude anti-ironism of sending explosives through the post was hardly Pynchon's style: his novels were his bombs, packaged charges of transformative argument delivered from a carefully guarded nowhere.
